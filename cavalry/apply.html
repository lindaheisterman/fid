Canine Cavalry

Application Form

This is an initial application that is required to be completed for review. Once your application is reviewed you will be contacted by a member of our team. If you are a match for the program additional information will be requested. You will be required to submit photos of your home, provide detailed information about your living and financial situation, complete referral letter process, provide contact information for a secondary support person, and attend a three week on-campus training program if your initial application is accepted.

Please allow 4-6 weeks for your initial application to be processed. All information provided is held in strict confidence. Please note that Canine Cavalry retains ownership of all Canine Cavalry dogs until their time of retirement, at which time each dog can be adopted out to their partnered veteran or an approved family within the Canine Cavalry network.
