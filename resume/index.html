About

With decades of experience across diverse industries, Linda brings a wealth of knowledge to every project. She has had the privilege of collaborating with small businesses, international organizations, and nonprofits, as well as working as an independent contractor. Her passion lies in helping individuals and businesses grow and thrive through innovative strategies and comprehensive services, including branding, graphic design, illustrations, marketing, writing, social media management, and music creation—from TV jingles to film placements and albums. Dedicated to transforming ideas into reality, Linda provides tailored solutions to meet each client’s unique needs. Whether assisting with simple illustrations, publications, or complex branding, music, and multimedia projects, she supports individuals, startups, growing ventures, and established businesses alike.
